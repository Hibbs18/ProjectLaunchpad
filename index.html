What is Project Launchpad?

Project Launchpad (PLP) is a one-stop support hub purpose-built for Canada's small to mid-sized charities. We equip under-resourced organizations with strategic guidance, accessible tools, and digital solutions to streamline operations, reduce burnout, and amplify impact—without stretching already limited budgets.
