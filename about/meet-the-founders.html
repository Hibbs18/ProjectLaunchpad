Meet the Founders

Project Launchpad is a collaboration between two organizations deeply committed to strengthening Canada's charitable sector.

HP

Harmonia Philanthropy

Strategic Partner & Sector Expertise

Harmonia Philanthropy brings deep expertise in the Canadian charitable sector, with a focus on capacity building, strategic planning, and sustainable growth for smaller organizations. Their team understands the unique challenges facing charities with limited resources and has spent years developing practical solutions that work in the real world.

With experience supporting dozens of charities across Canada, Harmonia Philanthropy contributes sector knowledge, fundraising expertise, and a network of trusted advisors to Project Launchpad. Their commitment to accessible, high-quality support for smaller charities is the foundation of everything we do.

Telos One

Technology Partner & Platform Development

Telos One is a technology company specializing in building platforms that solve complex organizational challenges. With expertise in software development, systems architecture, and user experience design, Telos One brings the technical capabilities needed to create a robust, scalable platform for Canada's charitable sector.

Their team is passionate about using technology to amplify social impact. For Project Launchpad, Telos One provides the platform infrastructure, security systems, and technical innovation that make sophisticated tools accessible to organizations of any size. They're committed to building technology that serves mission-driven work.
