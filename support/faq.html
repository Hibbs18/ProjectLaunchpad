Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Project Launchpad membership, resources, and support.

General Questions

What is Project Launchpad? +
Project Launchpad is a comprehensive platform designed specifically for smaller Canadian charities (those with annual revenues under $500,000). We provide access to strategic tools, expert guidance, proven templates, and professional support that help charities operate more effectively, fundraise more successfully, and amplify their impactâ€”all without the prohibitive costs typically associated with these resources.
Who is eligible to join Project Launchpad? +
Project Launchpad is designed exclusively for registered Canadian charities with annual revenues under $500,000. This includes both established organizations looking to enhance their operations and newer charities building their foundation. If you're a registered charity in Canada focused on maximizing your impact, you're likely a great fit for our community.
When will Project Launchpad launch? +
We're planning to launch in Fall 2025. By joining our waitlist now, you'll receive priority access when we launch, along with exclusive early-bird benefits and the opportunity to help shape the platform through our beta testing program.

Membership Questions

What's the difference between Launchpad Lite and Lift-Off Tier? +
Launchpad Lite (Free) provides essential resources including basic fundraising templates, security hygiene tips, policy libraries, grant readiness checklists, public webinars, and CRA compliance guides.

Lift-Off Tier ($99/month) includes everything in Launchpad Lite plus micro-consultations with experts, advanced toolkits, grant-readiness resources, Imagine Canada Grant Connect access, subsidized expert network access, priority webinar access, small-group coaching, student volunteer pool, and much moreâ€”delivering over $5,000 in annual value.
Can I switch between membership tiers? +
Yes! You can upgrade from Launchpad Lite to Lift-Off Tier at any time. If you're on the Lift-Off Tier and need to downgrade, you can do so at the end of your current billing cycle. We understand that charity budgets fluctuate, and we've designed our membership to be flexible.
Is there a contract or commitment period? +
No long-term contracts required! The Lift-Off Tier is billed monthly, and you can cancel at any time. We believe in earning your membership every month by delivering exceptional value. Launchpad Lite is free forever with no commitments.
How do I calculate the ROI of membership? +
The Lift-Off Tier delivers over $5,000 in annual value for just $99/month ($1,188/year). This includes access to Imagine Canada Grant Connect (normally $1,200/year), micro-consultations (typically $150-300 per session), subsidized expert access (15-25% savings), and hundreds of premium templates and tools. Most members see a 2.5x to 6x return on investment through improved fundraising, time savings, and avoided costs.

Resources & Tools

What types of resources are available? +
Our resource library includes fundraising templates and calendars, grant-writing toolkits, donor engagement strategies, social media templates, email marketing templates, policy libraries, cybersecurity tools, risk assessment frameworks, project management templates, volunteer management systems, compliance guides, and much more. All resources are specifically designed for smaller Canadian charities.
How often are new resources added? +
We continuously update and expand our resource library based on member feedback, sector trends, and emerging needs. Lift-Off Tier members receive notifications when new resources are added and have the opportunity to request specific resources they need.
Can I customize the templates for my charity? +
Absolutely! All our templates are fully customizable. They're designed to provide a strong foundation while allowing you to adapt them to your charity's specific needs, brand, and mission. We provide guidance on how to customize each resource effectively.

Support & Community

What are micro-consultations? +
Micro-consultations are focused, 30-minute sessions with sector experts where you can get specific guidance on challenges you're facing. Whether you need help with a grant application, fundraising strategy, governance question, or operational issue, our experts provide practical, actionable advice tailored to your situation. Lift-Off Tier members receive regular micro-consultation credits.
How does the vetted expert network work? +
Our Impact Network consists of pre-qualified professionals (consultants, accountants, lawyers, web developers, etc.) who have been vetted for quality and experience working with smaller charities. Lift-Off Tier members receive subsidized access to these experts at 15-25% below market rates, along with warm introductions and quality assurance.
Is there a community forum or networking opportunities? +
Yes! We facilitate peer learning through small-group coaching sessions, roundtables, and networking events. These opportunities allow you to connect with other charity leaders facing similar challenges, share best practices, and build valuable relationships within the sector.

Technical Questions

What technical requirements are needed? +
Project Launchpad is web-based and accessible from any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). You'll need a stable internet connection and a device (computer, tablet, or smartphone). Most resources are provided in common formats like PDF, Word, Excel, and Google Docs for easy access and editing.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We take data security seriously and follow industry best practices including encryption, secure authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA). We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.
