Governance & Policies

Transparency, accountability, and ethical operations are at the heart of Project Launchpad.

Our Commitment

As a platform serving Canada's charitable sector, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of governance, transparency, and ethical conduct. We're committed to operating with integrity and accountability in everything we do.

Governance Structure

Project Launchpad is a collaboration between Harmonia Philanthropy and Telos One, two organizations with complementary expertise in the charitable sector and technology innovation. Our governance structure ensures strategic oversight, operational excellence, and alignment with our mission to serve smaller Canadian charities.

Oversight & Leadership

Our leadership team brings decades of combined experience in charitable operations, technology development, and social impact. We maintain clear lines of accountability and regular review processes to ensure we're delivering on our commitments to members.

Core Principles

  • Transparency We operate openly, sharing information about our operations, finances, and decision-making processes with our community.
  • Accountability We take responsibility for our commitments and measure our performance against clear standards and member expectations.
  • Member-Centricity Member needs and feedback drive our decisions, from resource development to platform features.
  • Ethical Operations We conduct all business with integrity, respecting privacy, maintaining confidentiality, and upholding the highest ethical standards.
  • Continuous Improvement We regularly evaluate our performance and seek ways to better serve our members and the charitable sector.
  • Sector Stewardship We're committed to strengthening Canada's charitable sector and supporting the success of smaller organizations.

Member Feedback & Complaints

We value member feedback and take all concerns seriously. If you have feedback about our services, policies, or operations, or if you need to file a complaint, we have clear processes in place to address your concerns promptly and fairly.

Our comprehensive Member Feedback & Complaints Policy outlines multiple channels for providing feedback, our complaint investigation process, appeals procedures, and external escalation options.

Quick Contact

• General Feedback: feedback@projectlaunchpad.ca
• Service Issues: support@projectlaunchpad.ca
• Formal Complaints: complaints@projectlaunchpad.ca

Financial Transparency

Project Launchpad operates on a sustainable business model designed to serve our members while maintaining financial health. We're committed to:

• Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
• Regular financial reporting to stakeholders
• Responsible resource allocation focused on member value
• Long-term sustainability to serve the sector for years to come

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review our governance practices, policies, and operations to ensure we're meeting the evolving needs of our members and the charitable sector. This includes:

• Annual governance reviews
• Regular member satisfaction surveys
• Ongoing policy updates to reflect best practices
• Engagement with sector leaders and advisors
• Participation in relevant professional associations

Questions About Governance?

If you have questions about our governance structure, policies, or operations, we're here to help.

Contact us at governance@projectlaunchpad.ca
