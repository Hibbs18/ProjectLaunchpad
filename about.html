About Project Launchpad

Empowering smaller Canadian charities to achieve extraordinary impact through accessible tools, expert guidance, and strategic support.

Our Mission: To empower smaller Canadian charities with the tools, expertise, and systems they need to amplify their impact without the overhead.

Project Launchpad is an innovative platform designed to accelerate the development and deployment of charitable initiatives across Canada. We exist to bridge the gap between passion and capacity for smaller charities—those doing incredible work but often lacking the resources of larger organizations.

Our Approach

With a focus on collaboration, transparency, and scalability, Project Launchpad streamlines project management, fosters creativity, and supports continuous improvement. We offer two membership tiers designed specifically for Canada's smaller charities and nonprofits:

Launchpad Lite (Free): Essential resources including fundraising templates, security basics, policy libraries, and compliance guides to help you get started.

Lift-Off Tier ($99/month): Comprehensive support including micro-consultations, advanced toolkits, grant-readiness resources, vetted expert access, and much more—delivering over $5,000 in annual value.

Who We Serve

Whether you're a startup charity or an established organization looking to amplify your impact, Project Launchpad provides the infrastructure you need to succeed. We're here to help you navigate the journey from concept to launch, and from launch to sustainable growth.

Our Impact

Project Launchpad is a collaboration between Harmonia Philanthropy and Telos One. Built for impact, not profit, we're designed exclusively for Canada's smaller charities who are ready to transform their potential into measurable results.
