About Project Launchpad

Empowering smaller Canadian charities to achieve extraordinary impact through accessible tools, expert guidance, and strategic support.

Project Launchpad is an innovative platform designed to accelerate the development and deployment of charitable initiatives across Canada. Our Approach

With a focus on collaboration, transparency, and scalability, Project Launchpad streamlines project management, fosters creativity, and supports continuous improvement. We offer two membership tiers designed specifically for charities with annual revenues under $500,000:

Launchpad Lite (Free): Essential resources including fundraising templates, security basics, policy libraries, and compliance guides to help you get started.

Lift-Off Tier ($99/month): Comprehensive support including micro-consultations, advanced toolkits, grant-readiness resources, vetted expert access, and much more‚Äîdelivering over $5,000 in annual value.

Who We Serve

Whether you're a startup charity or an established organization looking to amplify your impact, Project Launchpad provides the infrastructure you need to succeed. We're here to help you navigate the journey from concept to launch, and from launch to sustainable growth.

Our Impact

Project Launchpad is a collaboration between Harmonia Philanthropy and Telos One. Built for impact, not profit, we're designed exclusively for Canada's smaller charities who are ready to transform their potential into measurable results.
